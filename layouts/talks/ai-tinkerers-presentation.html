LLMs are a New Medium of Communication

  • LLMs can compress and decompress intent
  • LLMs generate other media
  • LLMs muddle intent more than any other medium
  • More in depth treatment: Large AI models are cultural and social technologies

This is Completely Disorienting

  • Human communication evolved around effort-based trust
  • We can no longer gauge human effort behind communication
  • We prize connection, but now swim in a totally different media landscape
  • Some potential futures: human-only media, physical connection

Computing Quality & Safety

  • Traditional safety came from inherent human limits
  • Code took time, attention, and peer review
  • Human speed and attention provided natural safety bounds
  • If it compiled, you probably meant it

The Siren's Call

  • Speed of intent-to-reality is incredibly alluring. This is the stuff of science fiction
  • How do we maintain quality and safety?
  • Programming language that captures true intent
  • Preserve safety, reproducibility, and clarity
  • Refinement as the core workflow

Intentc: An Experimental Approach

  • Project exploring structured intent specification
  • Language for clearly specifying what you want
  • Maintains reproducibility and collaboration
  • Bridges human intent and machine execution
